10-Day Australia Highlights Group Tour

Explore Australia's most iconic cities and landscapes with a welcoming, fun-filled group.

Overview

Experience the perfect mix of nature, culture, and adventure on this 10-day guided group tour. From the world-famous Sydney Opera House to the golden beaches of the Gold Coast and the vibrant charm of Melbourne — this itinerary is built for travellers who want to see it all, without the stress.

Tour Highlights
  • Take in Sydney Harbour and the Opera House from the most spectacular viewpoints
  • Meet kangaroos and koalas up close at a native wildlife sanctuary
  • Thrill-seekers rejoice at the Gold Coast's world-class theme parks
  • Uncover Melbourne's iconic laneways, thriving arts scene, and legendary coffee culture
  • Journey along the awe-inspiring Great Ocean Road
  • Travel with experienced tour leaders and a great group of like-minded adventurers
Inclusions
  • 9 nights accommodation (3–4 star hotels)
  • Daily breakfast
  • All domestic transfers and inter-city travel
  • Theme park entry and guided tours
  • Airport pickup and on-the-ground local support
Exclusions
  • International flights
  • Visa fees
  • Personal expenses and meals not specified
  • Travel insurance
Itinerary Snapshot
Day 1–3: Sydney

City highlights tour, Bondi Beach, and iconic Harbour Bridge viewpoints.

Day 4–6: Gold Coast

Thrilling theme parks, sun-soaked beaches, and vibrant nightlife.

Day 7–10: Melbourne

City exploration and a full-day Great Ocean Road trip.
